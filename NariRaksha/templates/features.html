Features of NariRaksha

1. Emergency Alerts & SOS

  • Panic Button: Instantly sends alerts with location to pre-set contacts.
  • Auto-Dial Emergency Numbers: Calls police, fire, or ambulance automatically.
  • Silent Alarm: Sends alerts without making a sound (useful in dangerous situations).

2. Real-Time Location Tracking

  • Live GPS Tracking: Trusted contacts can monitor user location in real-time.
  • Geo-Fencing Alerts: Notify when entering high-risk or unsafe areas.
  • Route Sharing: Share route with friends/family during travel.

3. Community & Social Safety

  • Trusted Contact Network: Allow selection of family/friends to receive alerts.
  • Nearby Safe Zones: Maps showing police stations, hospitals, or well-lit public areas.
  • Community Reports: Users can report unsafe areas or incidents.

4. Safety Notifications

  • Check-in Reminders: Periodic prompts to confirm safety.
  • Unusual Behavior Alerts: Detect abnormal phone movement or lack of response.
  • Travel Safety Tips: Notify users about high-risk areas or safety practices.

5. Personal Safety Tools

  • Fake Calls: Trigger fake incoming call to escape uncomfortable situations.
  • Audio/Video Recording: Quick record to capture evidence discreetly.
  • Flashlight & Siren: For self-defense or drawing attention in emergencies.

6. Education & Awareness

  • Safety Tutorials: Guides on self-defense, first aid, and emergency response.
  • Legal Rights Information: Quick access to local laws regarding harassment/assault.
  • Helpline Directory: Access to counseling, medical, and legal services.

7. Smart Integration

  • Wearable Device Alerts: Link with smartwatches or fitness bands for SOS signals.
  • AI Risk Detection: Alerts when unusual movement patterns are detected (e.g., being followed).
  • Offline Mode: Send SOS messages even without active internet using SMS.
